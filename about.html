ABOUT ME


Anthony Maniscalco is a dedicated and experienced basketball skills trainer with a deep-rooted passion for the game. After having a successful high school career as an All-Conference player, Anthony continued to pursue the game of basketball in player development.


Over the years, Anthony has gained experience as a trainer by working under world-renowned NBA trainers, including Tim Grover (former trainer of Michael Jordan), Mike Procopio (founder of Hoop Consultants), and Jeff Pagliocca (current General Manager of the Chicago Sky). He assisted in workouts with NBA players such as Stephen Curry, Dwyane Wade, and many others. Anthony also comes from a strong family bloodline of basketball which includes his father Carl Maniscalco who played at Bradley University. His younger brother Sam who was a 1st team all state high school player and went on to have a successful college career at both Bradley University and University of Illinois. His older brother Jack who was all area high school player and led his conference in scoring.


With almost 2 decades of experience in player development, Anthony has honed a unique model of success that emphasizes the importance of basketball fundamentals. His approach is built around drills and techniques that not only enhance individual skills but also seamlessly translate into real-life game situations. This method has proven to be highly effective, helping young athletes reach their full potential on the court.


Whether working with beginners or advanced players, Anthony tailors his instruction to meet each athlete's specific needs, ensuring they receive the guidance and support necessary to excel. His passion for the game, combined with his extensive experience and proven track record, makes Anthony Maniscalco a sought-after instructor for aspiring basketball players in the Chicagoland area.
